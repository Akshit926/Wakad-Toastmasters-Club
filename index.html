About Us

Wakad Toastmasters Club is a vibrant community of aspiring speakers and leaders located in the heart of Wakad, Pune.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to help individuals overcome their fear of public speaking, our club has grown into a supportive community where members practice communication and leadership skills in a safe, encouraging environment.

We meet regularly in Wakad to share ideas, give speeches, receive constructive feedback, and celebrate each other's growth. Whether you're preparing for a presentation, improving your interview skills, or simply want to become a more confident communicator, we're here to help.
